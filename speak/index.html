Calling all speakers

Speaking at Hackcon benefits both you and our community. Hackcon is a great place to start if you have never spoken at a conference before. We work with our speakers to make sure they have everything they need to succeed. The Hackcon community is excited to learn from your experiences and hear your story. Speaking at Hackcon will be a valuable learning experience as well as a great way to meet other organizers.


If you have an interesting story or viewpoint to share, we want to hear from you! There are no specific qualification requirements for speaking at Hackcon; we’re looking instead for how your experience motivates your talk. Veteran organizer with lots of advice? Great! Never organized a hackathon but have insights from an attendee’s perspective? Also great!


Whether Hackcon is your first speaking opportunity or your hundredth, we’ll be providing you one-on-one speaker training to make sure your talk is as awesome as it can be. Our team members will work with you on everything from solidifying your idea and structuring your talk to polishing your slides and avoiding stagefright.

    • FAQ

    What types of talks are included at Hackcon?

    We like to maintain a balance between helpful tips and tricks for hackathon organizers and more forward-looking ideas about where hackathons are headed. Your talk should be motivated by our community and provide enough specifics for your allotted time.


    How long should my talk be?

    Hackcon has a variety of talk formats ranging from an hour long keynote to a 15 minute lightning talk. Our team will work with you to determine which will be the best fit for you and your content.


    Will my ticket be reimbursed?

    If you are selected to speak at Hackcon, your admission will be waived. Additionally, keynote speakers will have their travel reimbursed.
